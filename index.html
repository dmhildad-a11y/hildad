About HILD

HILD Defence and Aerospace was established to deliver modern engineering solutions for defence and homeland security. We design and develop unmanned vehicles, counter-drone systems (C-UAS), tactical payload drones and surveillance platforms that are rugged, reliable and built for field operations.

Our areas of expertise include system design, integration, AI-enabled sensing, and end-to-end support from R&D to manufacturing — with an emphasis on Make-in-India and self-reliance.
